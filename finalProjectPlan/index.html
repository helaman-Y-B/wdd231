Share Hike Site Plan

Site Name

The website will be called Share Hike

The Purpouse

The purpose of the ShareHike project is to help people find parks, mountains and hikes that they can take and appreciate the nature around them. Sometimes it can be hard to find a new place to explore through google maps and social media, but shareHike is here to solve this problem.

In ShareHike people share their experiences about a specific place that they visited so that others may know it and also enjoy that same place. This will also help those that own these places and want more visitors to help maintain the place, financially.

Scenarios

Scenario 1: The Casual Adventurer Looking for Inspiration

User: Lucas, a weekend hiker who is tired of going to the same two crowded trails near his city.

  • Goal: Find a new, unique nature spot for a Saturday morning hike without spending hours researching.
  • Question: How can the homepage showcase "hidden gem" highlights so visually and convincingly that Lucas instantly wants to click on them instead of closing the tab?

Scenario 2: The Local Explorer Using Filters

User: Clara, a university student who just moved to a new city and doesn't own a car.

  • Goal: Find a beginner-friendly hiking trail accessible by public transport in her specific city.
  • Question: What search filters (e.g., "bus route nearby", "easy difficulty") must be instantly visible on the places page to help Clara filter out impossible trails in seconds?

Scenario 3: The Passionate Backpacker Sharing an Adventure

User: Marcos, an experienced hiker who just returned from an incredible, lesser-known mountain trek.

  • Goal: Share his photos, trail conditions, and tips so others can safely enjoy the hidden gem.
  • Question: How simple and rewarding can we make the "Create Post" form so Marcos can effortlessly upload high-quality pictures and tag trail conditions without getting frustrated by a long form?

Scenario 4: The Eco-Park Owner Driving Traffic

User: Daniela, the manager of a private, protected nature reserve that relies on entry fees for conservation and maintenance.

  • Goal: Attract more visitors to the reserve to help fund trail maintenance and wildlife protection.
  • Question: How can the webpage distinguish Daniela's "Business Account" promotions (like discounts or news) from standard posts so they look professional and trustworthy to conservation-minded hikers?

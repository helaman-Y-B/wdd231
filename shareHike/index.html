Welcome to Share Hike!

Our purpose

The purpose of the Share Hike project is to help people find parks, mountains, and hikes that they can explore and enjoy while appreciating the nature around them. Sometimes it can be hard to find a new place to explore through Google Maps and social media, but Share Hike is here to solve this problem.

In Share Hike, people share their experiences about places they visited so others can discover and enjoy them too. This also helps the sites and destinations gain more visitors and support their preservation.
